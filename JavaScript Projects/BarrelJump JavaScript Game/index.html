Barrel Jump

Controls and Instructions

Use the 'A' and 'D' keys to move left and right.

Press 'W' to jump.

Hold W to bunny hop. Also, hold W when trying to jump on barrels. Sometimes, only trying to jump on top of them is not enough.

Jump on the barrels to score points and get bonuses. Follow InGame Instructions to finish the game.

If jumping on obstacles feels frustrating, just go on. It was made to be a bit challenging.

Use C to create a CheckPoint and V to teleport back to it!
