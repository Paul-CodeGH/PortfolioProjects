## Overview

DreamCart is a mobile-first wishlist and gift management application built with **Kotlin Native** using **Android Studio**, **ROOM Database**, and **JetPack Compose**. It solves the problem of scattered shopping ideas across multiple browser tabs by providing a centralized, organized platform for managing your shopping and gift-giving needs.

## The Problem & Solution

### Problem
Users often struggle to keep track of products they want to buy, leading to:
- Multiple open browser tabs
- Lost or forgotten shopping ideas
- Disorganized gift planning
- Stressful shopping experience

### Solution
DreamCart provides a unified platform where you can:
- Search and save products instantly
- Manually add items from any website via URL
- Organize your wishlist with filters and categories
- Plan gifts with integrated calendar functionality
- Ensure data persistence with secure database storage

## Key Features

### 🔍 **Search & Save**
Search through existing products and save them instantly to your library. Keep track of everything in one place.

### 🎁 **Smart Gift Management**
Mark items as gifts, assign them to specific people, and set due dates so you never miss a birthday or anniversary.

### ✏️ **Manual Entry**
Can't find it in search? Manually add products, specify prices, upload images, set categories, and paste external URLs.

### 📅 **Integrated Calendar**
Visualise your shopping timeline. The calendar tells you exactly when the next gift for someone needs to be bought.

### ⚡ **Filters & Organization**
Never struggle to find an item. Use our robust filtering system to sort your library by category, price, or date.

### 🔒 **Data Persistence**
Built with Kotlin, your data is synced to the database. Close the app, restart your phone, your wishlist remains safe.

### 🎉 **Bonus: Secret Santa System**
An optional enhanced feature for gift exchange management (not part of the official MVP).
